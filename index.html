Title of Project: Perceptions of Health Anxiety and Hypochondria in Young Individuals

Purpose of the Study

The purpose of this study is to understand how young individuals perceive health-related concerns and their own tendencies toward health anxiety or hypochondria.

What Participation Involves

Participants will view photos of doctors, provide their first impressions including trust and one-word reactions, and answer a brief questionnaire about their health attitudes.
